Aeon Game Design Document

OVERVIEW

The Aeon design document is based on an exercise for students applying to the CNAM-ENJMIN, so I thought it would be a good exercise to practice game design. The challenge being to outline every aspect of the game in only 10 pages.

Aeon is a story-based, exploration, puzzle-platformer, adventure game. The story revolves around an explorer and their companion trying to find an ancient artifact to save their home. The player takes control of the explorer and is tasked with traversing and exploring the dangers of the outside world as well as solving puzzles in order to reach the artifact.

Writing Aeon’s document challenged me to work under constraints. I thought about every aspect of game development. This ranges from creating a story and world, to the artistic direction the game should take, the specific game principles and gameplay loop, while tailoring the experience to its core audience.
