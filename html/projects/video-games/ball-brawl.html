Ball Brawl

Platform: - PC
Duration: 10 months
Team size: 1
Role: Solo project done to learn UE5

OVERVIEW

A 2-4 player party game where players throw balls at each other! The purpose of this project was to spend time to mess around with and learn Unreal Engine 5 and improve my skills with its Blueprint Scripting. I wanted to try and create a game that had the low skill floor and potential to have a high skill ceiling such as the Super Smash Bros. franchise. The game is completed and fully playable.
