Beyond Bounds

Platform: - Board game
Duration: 4 month
Team size: 4 students, 2 supervising professors
Role: Project Lead & Lead Designer

All screenshots and videos reflect the work of the entire team (art, code, audio, design…)

OVERVIEW

Survive a space mission, manage your resources and deal with whatever comes next! Created in cooperation with CERN IdeaSquare, where we developed a physical board game, and tested it in Geneva.

Beyond Bounds is a cooperative game where you are part of a team that is sent on a journey based on the IdeaSquare Planet (i2p) cycle that also matches with CERN’s DNA. The i2p cycle can be explained through the following image, with the journey back to planet earth being included in the game.

The game revolved around managing your supplies so that you and your crew (the players) could survive the space journey and eventually create a sustainable environment on the alien planet. A key aspect of the game was the crew dealing with moral conundrums known as “random events” where they would be presented with ethical dilemmas forcing them to make a difficult decision on how to proceed. These events were based on thought experiments such as the trolley problem. With each decision affecting the rest of their journey.

I was responsible for leading the project, concepting, designing, and playtesting the project, writing out all of the random events, and sketching out various versions of game pieces and the game board.
